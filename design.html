Stationhead Channels

  • product

  • ios

Stationhead is a place for music fans around the world to connect, listen live, and stream together. I worked with their team to design a new experience to help listeners discover new Stations and bring together related hosts and fans into a single space. The experience is called Channels and has become the stage for 15+ fandoms to connect since launching in early 2022.

Our solution to creating Channels was to build off the existing Stations view that Stationhead users already know. This kept things simple from the user-experience and build perspective. In order to make the experience of Channels feel unique from Stations we landed on a design that changes the complete look-and-feel with some simple changes. Each Channel has it's own logo and color to pair with the artist or group.

—

For a smooth transition from Station→Channel, and vice versa, there is a small messaging system that alerts hosts and listeners of planned scheduling changes.

—

Motion is a big part of communicating aspects of this experience. Understanding and communicting how interactions would feel meant producing higher fidelity prototypes throughout the project. Especially useful as a tool during handoff with the engineering team at Stationhead.
